About the Conference

IEEE International Conference on Computational Intelligence and Emerging Technologies -2023 (ICCIET-23) will be held in Apex Institute of Technology’s department Chandigarh University, Mohall, India, from December 2 to 3,2023. The main objective of the conference is to bring together the researchers from academia, research laboratories, and stakeholders from the industry. The conference will facilitate the presentation of the recent developments and exchange of practical experience in the field of Computational Intelligence and emerging technologies. The conference will encourage discussion, dissemination of information, exchange of ideas, and collaboration among the participants.

Proposed Themes

Track 1: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning & Applied Science

  • • Artificial Intelligence Systems
  • • Image, Speech, and Signal Processing
  • • Machine Learning
  • • Parallel and Distributed Architectures and Systems
  • • Internet Modelling, Semantic Web, and Ontologies
  • • Brain-Computer Interface
  • • Computational Intelligence for the IoT and Smart-X technologies
  • • Bio-informatics and Bio-Medical Simulation
  • • Big Data

Track 2: Robotics and Automation

  • • Soft Computing and Hybrid Soft Computing
  • • Control of Intelligent Systems
  • • Mechatronics, Robotics, Cybernetics

  • • Discrete Event and Real-Time Systems
  • • Mobile/Ad Hoc Wireless Networks, Sensor Placement, Target Tracking
  • • Virtual Reality, Visualization, and Computer Games

Track 3: Communication and Information Technologies

  • • Performance Engineering of Computer and Communication Systems
  • • High speed Communication
  • • E-Science and E-Systems

  • • Networking for Intelligent Mobility and Transport Infrastructures

Track 4: Advance Computing

  • • Cloud Computing
  • • Grid computing
  • • Scalable Computing
  • • Embedded Computing
  • • High Performance Computing

Track 5: Network Technologies

  • • Wireless and Mobile Communication
  • • Signal processing
  • • Cognitive Radio
  • • 5G/6G Network Evolution

OBJECTIVES

  • • To provide a platform for the exchange of views, useful insight, and learning for the benefit of industry, academia, consultant and research organizations, etc.
  • • To create an access to knowledge and research collaboration under one umbrella for the advancement of emerging technologies.
  • • To conduct activities to support indigenous technology promotion, development, utilization, and transfer of knowledge of AI enabled technologies.
  • • To promote innovations by students and entrepreneurs by conducting Hackathons, and Panel discussions.
